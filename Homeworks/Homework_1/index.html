VIRUSES!!!

A computer virus is a program designed to infect computers, replicate itself, and be passed on to other computers. Viruses are usually created with malicious intent, such as stealing information, locking down a computer, and corrupting the files of a computer.

Viruses are also known as malaware, malicious programs or software.
