VIRUSES!!!

A computer virus is a program designed to infect computers, replicate itself, and be passed on to other computers.

A type of malaware, or malicious software, viruses are usually created with malicious intent, such as stealing information, locking down a computer, and corrupting the files of a computer.

Some viruses act as soon as they infect an computer. Other viruses become active under certain conditions.
