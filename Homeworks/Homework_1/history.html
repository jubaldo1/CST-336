HISTORY!!!

Viruses had been around almost as long as the computer had been around. The idea of a computer virus had originally been produced in the 1940s and 1960s through John von Neumann's "Theory of Self-Producing Automata".

As the title suggests, the idea was that a program could be created to act similarily to an organic virus, ready to replicate and pass itself on to other hosts.
