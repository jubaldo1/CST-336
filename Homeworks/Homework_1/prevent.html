PREVENTION!!!

There are multiple ways to help keep your computer and other devices safe, such as:

  • Checking for the authenticity of a website or email source.
    • If you need further verification if an email if authentic, such as call for assistance or urgency, contact the sender of the email through other means.
  • Not downloading files from unknown sources.
  • Not going onto unsecure websites.
  • Not handing out sensitive information to unknown sources.
  • Not clicking on linkable ads or offers that seem too good to be true.
  • Having some type of antivirus program installed, such as Norton or Bitdefender.
